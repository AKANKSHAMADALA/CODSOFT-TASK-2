Course Specific Career Options

Students who enroll in the B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering programme will have access to a wide range of career opportunities. The rapid world of technological progress has its doors open to computer science experts. Career options include:

  • Software Developer
  • Game Developer
  • Database Administrator
  • Network Engineer
  • Data Analyst
  • Testing Engineer
  • Systems Analyst

Students who enroll in the B.Tech. Computer Science and Engineering (Data Science) programme will open a world of opportunities for themselves in this world driven by data-based innovations. Career options include:

  • Data Scientist
  • Data Analyst
  • Business Analyst
  • Data Engineer
  • Product Manager
  • Lead Data Scientist

Students who enroll in the B.Tech. Computer Science and Engineering (Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning) programme will assist graduates in developing a comprehensive understanding of the field, preparing them for a wide range of opportunities. Career options include:

  • Software Engineer
  • Software Tester
  • Application Developer
  • Information and Multimedia Designer
  • Medical Information Scientist
  • Technology Developer
  • IT Specialist

Students who enroll in the B.Tech. Computer Science and Engineering (Internet of Things - IoT) programme will be equipped to thrive in the interconnected world of IoT. Career options include:

  • IOT Solutions Architect
  • IOT System Developer
  • IOT Embedded Systems Engineer
  • IOT Security Consultant
  • IOT Network Analyst
  • IOT Data Scientist

Students who enroll in the B.Tech. Computer Science and Engineering (Cyber Security) programme will become a part of the emerging cybersecurity workforce. Career options include:

  • Cyber Security Manager
  • Security Specialist
  • Security Analyst and Engineer
  • Security Architect
  • Forensic Expert
  • Penetration Tester
